# E-Commerce Sales & Customer Funnel Analysis Dashboard

An interactive business intelligence dashboard built using **Power BI** to analyze e-commerce sales performance, customer activity, purchase behavior, and funnel conversion insights.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

The **E-Commerce Sales & Customer Funnel Analysis Dashboard** helps businesses monitor customer interactions and evaluate sales performance across different stages of the customer journey.

This dashboard provides insights into:

* Total Sales Revenue
* Purchase Trends
* Customer Funnel Stages
* Brand-wise Performance
* Category-wise Sales Analysis
* Monthly Session Trends
* Weekday Purchase Behavior

The project enables businesses to identify high-performing categories, customer engagement patterns, and conversion opportunities.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### 🔹 KPI Cards

* **Total Sales:** 2.87M
* **Purchases:** 10K
* **Total Users:** 9K

### 🔹 Funnel Analysis

Visual representation of customer journey stages:

* Users
* Sessions
* Purchases

Helps analyze conversion efficiency and drop-off points.

### 🔹 Sales by Category

* Displays total sales across product categories
* Identifies top-performing categories

### 🔹 Brand Performance Analysis

Scatter plot showing:

* Average Product Price
* Total Sales by Brand

Helps identify premium brands with high sales.

### 🔹 Purchase Trends by Weekday

* Compares purchasing activity across weekdays
* Useful for marketing and campaign planning

### 🔹 Monthly Session Analysis

* Tracks customer sessions over months
* Helps identify traffic trends

### 🔹 Interactive Filters

Users can dynamically filter data by:

* Event Type
* Brand
* Weekday

## 📈 Key Insights

* Electronics category generated the highest sales revenue.
* Purchases were highest on Tuesdays and Fridays.
* Funnel analysis indicates strong conversion from sessions to purchases.
* Some brands achieved high sales despite moderate average pricing.
* Monthly sessions showed a slight decline over time.
